## Overview
Automate repetitive IT tasks with Metis Automation.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Automation">
    ````
    Log in and navigate to Automation under your workspace menu.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new workflow">
    ````
    Click "New Workflow", choose a template or start from scratch.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tasks and triggers">
    ````
    Select tasks (e.g., backups, user onboarding) and set conditions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate">
    ````
    Save and activate the automation. Monitor under 'Active Workflows'.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
