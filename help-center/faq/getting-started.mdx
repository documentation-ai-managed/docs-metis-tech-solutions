## Overview

Sign up for access, configure your workspace, and connect IT systems for transformation projects.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://portal.metis-tech.com/register`
2. Choose your business type and enter details
3. Set up your company workspace
4. Add your team members
5. Connect your existing IT systems

You're ready to start leveraging Metis Tech Solutions for your digital transformation needs.
