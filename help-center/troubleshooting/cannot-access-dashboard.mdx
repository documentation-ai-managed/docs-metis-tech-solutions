---
title: Cannot Access Metis Dashboard
description: Fix login/dashboard access issues.
---

## Symptoms
- Login page loads but dashboard does not
- Error: "Dashboard not available"

## Common Causes
- Incorrect credentials
- Browser cache errors
- Account not activated

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Clear your browser cache and cookies. Close and reopen your browser.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check account activation">
    ````
    Ensure your email is verified. Check your inbox for activation email.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Click "Forgot password" on login and follow instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
