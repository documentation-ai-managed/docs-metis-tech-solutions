## Onboarding Process

Follow these steps to complete onboarding efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Schedule Initial Assessment" icon="calendar">
    After contact, MTS sends a Calendly link. Book a 30-minute call.

    Review the [assessment agenda](https://metis.tech/docs/assessment) beforehand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding Forms" icon="clipboard-list">
    Receive a secure onboarding packet via email. Fill out:

    | Form | Purpose | Deadline |
    |------|---------|----------|
    | Client Profile | Company details | 48 hours |
    | NDA Agreement | Confidentiality | Before call |
    | Tech Audit Checklist | Current stack | 72 hours |

    Sign digitally using the provided DocuSign link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attend Kickoff Meeting" icon="video">
    Join the video call on Zoom. Discuss goals and roadmap.

    ````bash
    # Test your setup beforehand
    echo "Zoom link: https://metis.zoom.us/j/123456789"
    # Ensure microphone and camera work
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Collaboration Tools" icon="settings">
    Install recommended tools for seamless communication.
  </Step>
</Steps>
